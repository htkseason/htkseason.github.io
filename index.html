Zhixiang Min

I am a Ph.D. student (2018-) in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology, advised by Professor Enrique Dunn. I spent summer internships at Zillow and NEC Labs.

My research interest lies in 3D computer vision (SfM, SLAM, localization, reconstruction) and recently image-based rendering. My works during PhD focus on closing the gap between deep learning methods and 3D vision problems. Specifically, I design localization algorithms that ultilizes deep learning to improve challenging conditions and solve ill-pose problems, while, importantly, maintaining their geometric interpretabilities.
